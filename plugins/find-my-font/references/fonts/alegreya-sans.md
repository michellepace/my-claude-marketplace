# Synopsis: Alegreya Sans

Humanist sans serif with calligraphic rhythm. Part of the Alegreya "super family" designed for literature and long-form reading.

![Alegreya Sans 300](images/alegreya-sans.jpg)

## Key Characteristics

- **Classification:** Humanist sans serif
- **Character:** Calligraphic feeling with dynamic, varied rhythm; produces "ludic and harmonious" paragraphs through subtle letterform diversity
- **Intended use:** Long-form body text
- **Family:** Alegreya super family — matched [serif sibling](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Alegreya), [small caps companion](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Alegreya+Sans+SC)
- **Adoption (2026-03-21):** 71.2M weekly serves, 259K+ websites

## Technical

- **Variable font (0):** Not available
- **Weights:** 100, 300, 400, 500, 700, 800, 900
- **Styles:** Normal + Italic at each weight

## Kupferschmid Matrix

- **Layer 1 Skeleton:** Dynamic (open apertures, diagonal axis)
- **Layer 2 Flesh:** Contrast Sans (calligraphic stroke modulation, no serifs)
- **Confidence:** High — clear calligraphic modulation and open forms
