# Synopsis: Faustina

Editorial serif designed by Alfonso Garcia for Omnibus-Type as part of the Press Series. Crafted for editorial typography in books, newspapers, and magazines, in print and online.

## Key Characteristics

- **Classification:** Editorial serif
- **Character:** Press Series typeface designed for editorial typography
- **Intended use:** Editorial — books, newspapers, and magazines, in print and online
- **Family:** Part of the Omnibus-Type Press Series
- **Adoption (2026-04-27):** 24.0M weekly serves, 16,300+ websites

## Technical

- **Variable font (1):** Weight (`wght`) 300–800
- **Weights:** 300, 400, 500, 600, 700, 800
- **Styles:** Normal + Italic at each weight
- **Subsets:** latin, latin-ext, vietnamese
