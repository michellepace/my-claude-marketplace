# Synopsis: Montserrat

Geometric sans serif designed by Julieta Ulanovsky, inspired by old posters and signs in the traditional Montserrat neighbourhood of Buenos Aires. Rescues the beauty of urban typography that emerged in the first half of the twentieth century.

## Key Characteristics

- **Classification:** Sans serif (geometric, urban-signage inspired)
- **Character:** Letters with work, dedication, care, colour, contrast, light and life — drawn from urban typography of early twentieth-century Buenos Aires
- **Intended use:** Not specified by the source
- **Family:** Has two sister families — Montserrat Alternates and Montserrat Subrayada ('Underlined')
- **Adoption (2026-04-29):** 12.2B weekly serves, 13.2M+ websites

## Technical

- **Variable font (1):** Weight (`wght`) 100–900
- **Weights:** 100, 200, 300, 400, 500, 600, 700, 800, 900
- **Styles:** Normal + Italic at each weight
